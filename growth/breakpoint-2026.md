## Booth / Presence Strategy

**Option A: Full booth** (~$5K–15K depending on tier)
- Branded space with live product demo on tablet/laptop
- Two team members minimum (CEO + Growth or CEO + Engineer)
- QR code for instant waitlist signup with `ref=breakpoint` tracking

**Option B: Satellite presence (no booth)**
- Roaming with printed one-pagers and QR stickers
- Host or co-host a side event / dinner
- Book 1:1 meetings with target B2B contacts in advance

**Recommendation:** Start with Option B budget-secured, upgrade to A if funding allows. The key deliverable is face-to-face conversations, not foot traffic.
