## Section 1: Describe your startup idea (150–200 words)

UAE residents and expatriates hold hundreds of billions of dollars in local bank accounts earning 0.5–2.5% per year. Institutional lending markets — the same platforms used by banks and asset managers to deploy capital — generate 5% or more on equivalent dollar deposits. The gap between what a person earns in a standard savings account and what institutions earn on the same capital has never been wider.

Vault closes that gap. It is a savings app that accepts dollar deposits from everyday UAE residents and routes them into vetted institutional lending markets, currently earning approximately 5.4% per year. Users open an account, deposit money in dollars, earn fees from borrowers, and withdraw anytime — no lock-in, no minimum balance, no financial expertise required.

The infrastructure operates through the Sentora PYUSD vault, one of the largest dollar lending facilities in the world, with over $3 billion deployed by institutional borrowers. Vault makes this infrastructure accessible to retail savers in the UAE for the first time, through a simple mobile-first product that requires no technical knowledge whatsoever.
